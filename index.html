About

I'm looking to take the perspective I've built as part of one of the largest EDA vendors in the semiconductor industry and apply it in environments where the pace is faster and the leverage is higher. That could mean deepening on the technical side as a physical design engineer, PD CAD engineer, or RTL engineer at a fabless or hyperscaler chip program. It could also mean stepping into venture capital, semiconductor research, or industry analyst roles where the work is about understanding where the next decade of silicon is headed. I'm comfortable in both worlds and interested in opportunities that combine them.
